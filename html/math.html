Mathematics Hub

This is a hub page for mathematics content hosted on this site. Here you may find lesson plans, exercises, notes, ideas, failures, and more. When I get around to adding it, anyway.

The dropdown menu above gives links to several different pages. I'll briefly mention each here.

First on the list is Algebra, where I sort topics including everything from elementary and intermediate algebra courses, linear algebra, as well as abstract algebra and the algebra of sets and other mathematical objects. In this section (and in [eventually] all others) you can find notes, worked examples, tips, and more.

Next is Calculus, where I cover topics ranging from differentiation and integration in one variable and multiple variables, infinite series, and matrix calculus (some prerequisites of which are covered in the Algebra section).

Finally comes Number Theory, which covers many topics essential to our work in other sections: primality, the Fundamental Theorem of Arithmetic, divisibility, and some aspects of algebraic systems. This is a particular area of interest for me!
